The main features of the EXPERT TUITION CENTER are as:

Spacious Air Conditioned Classrooms:

Expert Tuition Center have spacious classroom fitted with AC's. We enroll very limited number of students per class, thus providing us a better chance to achieve one to one interaction with students and lecturers. Faculty Members can focus on the weakness of a particular student and work on it.

Experienced Faculty Members:

In Expert Tuition Center we will give you the best among the bests to extract the best in you. The faculty here are well experienced, committed and experts in their respective field. They are friendly to students and are affable at any time.

Study Materials

The study materials/ assignments, we provide is considered to be the fine extract of the respective topics by the students and experts. More stress is given to Objective-type questions, as most of the competitive exams will be of Objective type.

Quality Teaching at Affordable Prices

Expert Tuition Center does not consider teaching as a business. We give tuitions for needy at affordable price. Our coaching fee is minimal as compared to other tuition centers, without compromising in the quality of teaching. We also have the facility of scholarship for the merited students who have the zeal for knowledge.

Lab Facilities

Expert Tuition Center also has a well equipped laboratory, which enable the students to practice the practical experiments and let them to know the concepts thoroughly.

Calm Environment

Our center is located away from the bustling market, so that every student can concentrate on studies. There is no distractions.

Weekly Seminars & Tests

Every week we conduct a seminar/ test on particular topic. The seminars are conducted either by our faculty or from the experts. This will enhance the knowledge of the students. It will help the students to understand the concepts thoroughly.

Periodic Tests

Frequent tests are conducted in order to keep the students focused on studies. Test marks are considered seriously and reports are sent to the parents. Attendance is strictly maintained and discussed with the wards of the students on the regular basis.

Counseling Facilities

Expert Tuition Center also guide the students about their career goals by inviting counseling experts from time to time and let the students avail the solution to their problems.
